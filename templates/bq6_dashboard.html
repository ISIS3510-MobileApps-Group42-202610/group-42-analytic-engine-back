Methodological Definitions

  • Buyer contact: first first_message_sent event in a conversation.
  • Seller response: first seller message_sent after buyer contact.
  • Response time: seller first reply timestamp minus buyer first message timestamp.
  • Only completed conversations with both buyer and seller events are measured.
