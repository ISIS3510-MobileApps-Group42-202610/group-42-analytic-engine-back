Methodological Definitions

  • Category / Product: extracted from the metadata.category and metadata.product fields of each transaction_completed event.
  • Semester: derived from metadata.semester (e.g. 2025-A for Jan–Jun, 2025-B for Jul–Dec).
  • Revenue: sum of metadata.selling_price across all completed transactions per category.
  • Stacked bar: shows how each category contributes to overall volume in each academic semester.
