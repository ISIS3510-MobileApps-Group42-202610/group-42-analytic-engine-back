Methodological Definitions

  • Category: from transaction_completed.metadata.category, emitted by the Flutter app when a purchase is completed.
  • Price suggestion usage: listing_created.metadata.price_suggestion_accepted for the same listing_id, emitted when the seller creates a listing. Listings with no matching creation event are counted as "no data".
  • Academic calendar: derived from the transaction timestamp using the Colombian university calendar (UniAndes schedule). Periods: Winter Break (Dec 16–Jan 20), Sem 1 Start (Jan 21–Feb 28), Sem 1 Mid (Mar–Apr), Sem 1 Finals (May–Jun 20), Summer Break (Jun 21–Jul 20), Sem 2 Start (Jul 21–Aug 31), Sem 2 Mid (Sep–Oct), Sem 2 Finals (Nov–Dec 15).
  • Adoption rate: listings where price_suggestion_accepted = true divided by total sold listings with a known suggestion status.
