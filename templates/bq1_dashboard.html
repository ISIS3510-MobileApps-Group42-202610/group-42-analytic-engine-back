Methodological Definitions

  • The hotspot label uses code_location first, then feature_name, then crash_signature.
  • Percentages represent the share of crash reports, not an exposure-adjusted crash rate.
  • When device or OS metadata is missing, the dashboard groups those records under Unknown device or Unknown OS.
  • The breakdown tables help pinpoint whether one crash hotspot is isolated to a specific device family or OS release.
